---
name: research-synthesis
description: Improve research, trend, report, and feasibility answers with stronger synthesis and structure.
---

Use this skill when the user is asking for research hotspots, trends, reports, plans, comparisons, or feasibility analysis.

Core rules:
1. Start with a direct conclusion or synthesis sentence.
2. Group the answer into 2-4 high-signal themes instead of dumping disconnected points.
3. Prefer synthesis, tradeoffs, and next-step guidance over generic filler.
4. Keep the structure decision-useful and concise.
5. If evidence exists, stay grounded in it and preserve key numbers.
6. Avoid mechanical heading stacks or decorative separators unless the user explicitly asks for a structured report.
